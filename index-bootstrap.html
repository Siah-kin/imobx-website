How Bonzi Connects

The Problem We're Solving

1.6 billion people lack adequate housing — SDG 11

Traditional rent is broken:

  • Middleman extraction: Landlords capture 100% of rent, families build zero equity
  • Bank gatekeeping: 82% excluded from mortgages (no credit history, informal income)
  • Circular fees: RWA platforms charge percentage-based fees that compound endlessly
Our approach: Flat fees per transaction (not percentages), rent payments build equity toward ownership, government-verified construction costs (SINAPI), no bank required.

How It Works

Test case: Jardim Guadalupe, São Paulo

SINAPI-VERIFIED TRANSPARENT COSTS

Construction: $27,777 per unit

1. DAO buys land

Transparent treasury, multisig approval for large purchases

2. Build with SINAPI verification

Government cost index ensures no middleman markup. >10% variance triggers multisig review.

3. Families rent-to-own

Monthly rent builds equity. After 10-15 years, ownership transfers on-chain. No bank approval needed.

4. Flat fees sustain DAO

$10 buy / $15 sell per swap (not percentage). DAO treasury grows without extracting from families.

Status: Research phase. We're testing this model in Brazil first, then adapting for other jurisdictions (US: RSMeans, UK: BCIS).

How Bonzi Connects to IMOBX

Test cooperation tools first, then apply them to housing

Bonzi is our testing ground for the DAO toolkit that will power IMOBX.

Cooperation scoring (CPI)

Track participation signals (help, education, respect, ownership). Test whether this creates healthier communities than token-voting alone.

Trust oracle

B2B API for querying wallet reputation. IMOBX will use this to verify DAO member contributions before governance decisions.

Flat fee economics

$10 buy / $15 sell per transaction (not percentage). Proving this model works before applying to rent-to-own.

RACI governance

Clear decision roles (Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed). Prevents centralization + chaos.
